VR Escape Room

A fully interactive VR escape room built in Unity where players collect items, solve puzzles, and navigate immersive environments using hand-tracked VR interaction.

Role: Lead Developer & VR System Designer

Designed and developed the escape room environment, implemented gameplay puzzles and interaction systems, integrated team assets, and converted the project into a fully interactive VR experience in Unity.

Features

  • Interactive VR object
  • Puzzle-solving mechanics
  • Item collection system
  • Hand-tracked player
  • Immersive room design

Improved Unity and C# development skills.

Gained experience designing VR interactions and gameplay systems.
